Task
Highlight: Emphasizing or selecting specific content by changing its appearance.
Position: Making previously hidden content like the whole visualization or part of it (like data marks) visible.
Disappear: Removing the visualization from view, making it invisible.
Move：Relocating the visualization on the screen to a different position.
Zoom: Enlarging or reducing the visualization size.
Split/Merge:Breaking down data marks (like bubbles) within the visualization into components or merging individual data marks into an aggregated form.
